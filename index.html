Set up Yo Human

Get Claude Code to text your phone when it needs you — and reply with your next instructions from anywhere. About 5 minutes. You'll need a Mac with Claude Code installed.

Private preview — rough edges expected

1On your phone

  1. Tap your TestFlight invite → install TestFlight, then Yo Human.
  2. Open Yo Human and allow notifications.
  3. Copy your pairing code (looks like yohuman-you-mac).

2On your Mac — one time (no Terminal)

Open the Claude app on your Mac and click the </> Code tab. Paste this message to Claude (swap in your code from step 1) and send it:

Please set up Yo Human on this Mac by running this command:
curl -fsSL https://gitfischerai.github.io/yohuman-get/install.sh | bash -s -- YOUR-CODE

Claude runs it for you. If it asks permission to run the command, click Allow. You'll see green ✓ lines ending in ✅ Done. That's the whole setup.

First time only: the </> Code tab needs Claude Code (free — claude.com/claude-code). If Claude says jq is missing, reply: please install jq with: brew install jq.

3That's it — drive it from your phone

  1. Open Yo Human on your phone, tap the ✏️, then type or speak a task and send it.
  2. Your Mac starts working automatically. When it finishes or needs you, your phone buzzes.
  3. Reply in the app — type or tap the mic — and it keeps going, from anywhere.

No Terminal, ever. The engine runs quietly in the background and restarts itself, even after you reboot. Just keep the Claude app open on your Mac.

It's all local. Work runs on your Mac with your own Claude — Yo Human never runs the AI, and nothing of your work goes to a third-party AI. No extra AI cost.

Trouble? No buzz → check the app shows your Mac paired and phone Notifications are on (not in a Focus/DND window). Nothing happens after you send a task → make sure the Claude app is open on your Mac. Anything else → text Brian a screenshot.
