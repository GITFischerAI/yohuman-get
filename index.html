Set up Yo Human

Yo Human lets the AI on your computer (Claude Code) buzz your phone when it needs you — and you can text or talk back to keep it working, even when you're away from your desk.

⏱️ About 5–10 minutes · one time · no “Terminal” needed

What you need before you start

  • 📱 An iPhone
  • 💻 A Mac computer (Apple laptop or desktop)
  • The Claude app already installed on that Mac, and you're signed in
  • The invite email from Brian (it's called a “TestFlight” invite)
📱

Part 1 — On your phone

First we get the Yo Human app onto your iPhone.

1Open Brian's invite email and tap the button inside it.

This opens an Apple app called TestFlight — that's just how Apple lets you try apps before they're in the App Store.

If your phone asks you to install TestFlight first, tap Get / Install, then go back to Brian's email and tap the invite button again.

2Install Yo Human.

Inside TestFlight you'll see Yo Human. Tap Install (or Accept, then Install). Wait for it to finish.

3Open Yo Human.

Tap the Yo Human app to open it.

4Tap “Allow” when it asks about notifications.

A box pops up asking if Yo Human can send you notifications. Tap Allow.

Why: Notifications are the buzz on your phone. Without them, your computer can't tell you when it needs you.

That's it for the phone (for now). Set it down and go to your Mac.

💻

Part 2 — On your Mac (one-time setup)

You will not use anything scary. You'll just talk to Claude, and Claude does the setup for you.

1Open the Claude app on your Mac.

It's the same app you use to chat with Claude.

2Click the tab that says </> Code.

Look along the top or side of the Claude app for a small tab labeled </> Code. Click it.

What this is: this is “Claude Code” — the version of Claude that can do things on your computer, like set up Yo Human for you.

3Paste this message to Claude.

Copy the whole box below into the Claude Code chat — exactly as it is, nothing to change — and press Return to send it.

Please set up Yo Human on this Mac by running this command,
then show me the channel code it prints at the end:
curl -fsSL https://gitfischerai.github.io/yohuman-get/install.sh | bash
4Click “Allow” if Claude asks permission.

Claude might ask “Can I run this command?” Click Allow (or Yes).

Why: you're giving Claude permission to do the setup for you. That's the whole point.

5Wait for the green check marks — and your channel code.

Claude shows a few lines with green ✓ marks, ending with ✅ Done and a box like this:

📲 LAST STEP — connect your phone:

   YOUR CHANNEL CODE:   yohuman-yourname-yourmac

What this is: the channel code is like your Mac's phone number. In the next step you'll save it in the app so this Mac can buzz this phone.

If Claude says something is missing (like jq), just reply: “please install what's missing and try again.”

6Pick up your phone and enter that code.

On your phone: open Yo Human → tap Add channel (if you don't see it on the first screen, look under Manage channels) → type the channel code exactly as Claude showed it → save.

When the channel appears in your list, setup is finished. 🎉

✅

Part 3 — How to use it

This is the fun part — and it's all from your phone.

1Keep the Claude app open on your Mac.

Yo Human works while Claude is open on your computer. You can walk away — just don't quit the app.

2Start a task from your phone.

Open Yo Human, tap the ✏️ (pencil), then type a task or tap the microphone and say it. Tap send.

Example: “Add a contact form to my website.”

3Go do your thing.

Your Mac starts working. When it finishes — or has a question — your phone buzzes. 📳

4Reply right from your phone.

Open the buzz and type or talk to answer. Your computer keeps going. You can be anywhere.

🛠️ If something doesn't work

My phone never buzzed.

Open Yo Human and check it says your Mac is paired / connected. Make sure your phone's notifications are on and it isn't in Do Not Disturb / Focus mode.

Nothing happened after I sent a task.

Make sure the Claude app is still open on your Mac.

Anything else, or it just looks weird.

Take a screenshot and text Brian. This is a first test — finding rough spots is exactly the job. 🙏

It's all local & private. The work runs on your own Mac with your own Claude — Yo Human never runs the AI and none of your work goes to a third-party AI. No extra AI cost.
